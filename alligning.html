Scientific name: Panthera tigris. Subspecies: Historically ~9; extant commonly recognized: Bengal, Indochinese, Malayan, Amur (Siberian), South China (critically low), Sumatran; tiger-range extinctions reduced diversity. Range: Native to Asia — India, Southeast Asia, Russian Far East, Sumatra; fragmented populations. Size: Males 90–310 kg depending on subspecies; length (head+body) ~2–3.3 m; females smaller.
